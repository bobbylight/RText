Changing the Background

In RText, you have the option of either choosing a solid color for your background (what appears behind any text), or an image, such as a picture you took on your digital camera.


To change the background, from the Format menu, select Background. A dialog will appear allowing you to select a color to use as your background, or browse your computer for an image to use as your background.

Note that any image selected must be a supported format, or no background will be used. Currently supported image formats include GIF and JPG.
