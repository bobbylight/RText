Replacing Text

There are three ways to replace text in RText:

  1. Click on the Replace button on the Toolbar.
  2. Go to the Edit menu, then select Replace.
  3. Press Ctrl+H (or whatever shortcut you have defined for this action).

The Replace Dialog will appear, allowing you to enter the text to search for and the text to replace with.


For a tutorial on searching with regular expressions in RText, please see Using Regular Expressions.
