Playing Back the Last Macro

To playback the last macro, do one of the following

  1. Go to the Macros menu, then select Playback Last Macro.
  2. Press Ctrl+Shift+M.

This will play the most recently-recorded macro (or the one most recently selected from the Load Macro submenu of the Macros menu) in the current text document. All edits will be made simultaneously, and can be undone with a single undo.

See also: Recording macros, Recording temporary macros, Stop recording a macro
